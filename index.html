Women Techmakers Buea (WTM Buea) create a diverse community in which women can connect, be inspired, and encourage each other to realize their passions. This program provides visibility,community and resources for women in Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM).We are continually contributing to open source and empowering women around the Buea area.
